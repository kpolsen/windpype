Parameters:

  • data_df_2 (dataframe) – Dataframe 2
  • method (str) – Which method to use for combining the dataframes, default: ‘append’ - ‘append’: add data_df_2 onto data_df_1 - ‘merge’: merge the two together, on time stamps - ‘stitch’: append the latter on the first dataframe, cutting away overlapping time stamps from the latter
